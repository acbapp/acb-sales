What is ACB?

ACB, or adjusted cost base, is a method for calculating capital gains and losses ond sales of a certain glass of goods known as identical properties, among other things. The most common example would be units of a mutal fund, cryptocurrency, or some other non-unique item. It also includes property. Calculating the ACB takes time and effort, but this app desires to make it simple to track your gains and losses over time, automatically generate appropriate tax forms, and more, all for FREE!
